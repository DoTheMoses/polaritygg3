LEGIONS
Legions is a clan based event series integrated into regular tournament series in order to push ideals of teamwork, growth, friendships, rivalries, and exciting new content. The simplest description is that competitors become part of a clan, and their results in tournament attribute to that clan's success. The idea was contrived from theory to make 1v1 e-sports more exciting to everyone, by increasing the value of every individual player, but also the value of the viewers and anyone who enjoys the content.
